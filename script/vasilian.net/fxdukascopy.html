FX-Dukascopy


Add 2 shortcuts for JForex client (Demo & Live) from Dukascopy

cd /usr/share/applications
touch dukascopy-demo.desktop
touch dukascopy-live.desktop

cat > dukascopy-demo.desktop << EOF
[Desktop Entry]
Encoding=UTF-8
Name=Dukascopy JForex Demo
Comment=Dukascopy JForex Demo Account
Exec=javaws https://demo-login.dukascopy.com/client/demo/jclient/jforex.jnlp
Icon=/usr/share/icons/dukascopy-live.png
Categories=Application;
Version=1.0
Type=Application
Terminal=0
EOF

cat > dukascopy-live.desktop << EOF
[Desktop Entry]
Encoding=UTF-8
Name=Dukascopy JForex Live
Comment=Dukascopy JForex Live Account
Exec=javaws https://live-login.dukascopy.com/client/live/jclient/jforex.jnlp
Icon=/usr/share/icons/dukascopy-live.png
Categories=Application;
Version=1.0
Type=Application
Terminal=0
EOF

Add the icons. Meantime these were removed from sites.google.com

cd /usr/share/icons
wget https://sites.google.com/site/grayasm/fx/dukascopy-demo.png
wget https://sites.google.com/site/grayasm/fx/dukascopy-live.png
chown -v root:root dukascopy*.png
chmod 644 dukascopy*.png
